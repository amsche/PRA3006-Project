Welcome to X-Check! X-Check aims to make information accessible to the public. Our site allows users to enter an infectious disease, see the side effects of that disease, and find one or more drugs that treat those side effects.

X-Check is a student-led project created for entertainment purposes only. We do not claim to provide medical advice. Please see a doctor for a proper diagnosis and prescription.

Our Team

Our team is made up of Alina, Jens, Matt, and Nina. We are programming students at Maastricht University. Our goal was to make a functioning website focusing on the life sciences.
